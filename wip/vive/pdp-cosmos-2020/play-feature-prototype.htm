The forefront of VR headset design.

Unmatched graphics quality.

Enjoy VR content through a 2880 x 1700 combined pixel resolution—the most impressive VIVE visual resolution yet. All-new LCD panels reduce the distance between pixels, minimizing the screen-door effect.

Quick & easy setup.

Inside-out tracking enables plug-and-play portability. Pairs with a wide-array of VR-ready PC desktops and laptops. No base stations needed.
