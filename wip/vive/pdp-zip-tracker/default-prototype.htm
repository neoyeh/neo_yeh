A hint of a scowl. A sneer. A smile. VIVE Facial Tracker captures expressions and gestures with precision through 38 blend shapes across the lips, jaw, teeth, tongue, cheeks, and chin. Pair with VIVE Pro Eye for a whole-face tracking experience.
