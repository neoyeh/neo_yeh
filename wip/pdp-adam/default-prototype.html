Lifelike expressions. Real-time, natural interactions. Unmatched virtual presence. Your all-in-one VR experience is primed and ready to go up a notch.
Automatically adjusts to your viewing sweet spot.
The tracker intelligently detects your interpupillary distance (IPD) and automatically calibrates the headset for optimal viewing comfort and visual clarity.2

Easily share your VIVE XR Elite with multiple users without manually adjusting the IPD slider each time.
Your eyes are the window to next-level XR.
The two sensors, with infra-red illuminators, capture your gaze direction, pupil size and position, and eye openness.3 The tracker further unlocks dynamic foveated rendering to optimize processing performance for the graphics in your line of sight.4

Natural avatar interactions with realistic eye movements, gaze-based control, GPU optimization, and advanced insights based on user-specific data are several of the many applications.5
Beyond verbal communication.
The tracker captures expressions through 38 blend shapes across your lips, teeth, tongue, cheeks, and chin at a 60 Hz tracking rate, enabling near-zero latency between lifelike facial movements and voice audio.

Communicate and interact with non-verbal cues just as you would in the physical world.
Realism with expressive, insightful interactions. VIVE Full Face Tracker unlocks incredible immersive experiences.
